Apple Cinnamon Protein Oatmeal

Description

A breakfast classic, this is just another copy of the wheel - no reinventing here.

The same warm, spiced, sweet oatmeal that tastes just like apple pie, but made more satiating and muscle building for those of us looking to stay full until lunch or hit the day's protein goal.

Super easy to throw together after a morning gym session, and even easier to throw together the night before to take on the go, this is the perfect and macro friendly breakfast.

From my table to yours,

Roxana

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup old fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 cup liquid of your choice: water for lower cals, filtered milk for higher protein
  • 1/4 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 scoop protein powder of your choice - cinnamon adjacent flavor would work best, but vanilla or even unflavored work too!
  • ground cinnamon
  • ground nutmeg
  • ground cloves
  • vanilla extract
  • Optional: maple syrup or honey
  • Optional: peanut butter
